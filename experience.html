The Experience

Main Salon

Open 7 days a week for lunch and dinner, East Asian Bistro in Guaynabo can sit up to 328 people. The restaurant offers a large dining room with a complete Asian menu and Sushi Bar, Teppanyaki tables for any occasion, bar area and a private Activity Room. If you need a venue in Guaynabo City for a private luncheon, meeting or presentation, a special occasions such as a rehearsal dinner, wedding reception, birthday or anniversary, the Activity Room is the perfect setting for endless possibilities.

To start your dining journey, our chefs have created many tapas for your enjoyment, from the popular dumplings to the exotic flavor of crab rangoons. The Chinese menu is made up of our famous Peking duck, Hong Kong pan-fried noodles, creamy pearl shrimps and our customers’ favorite, orange chicken. The menu also features some of the best prepared and most succulent Thai food in Puerto Rico, such as our enticing Pad Thai Noodles and the Satay Chicken or Beef.

From our Japanese menu, you will experience one of the most complete selections of sushi available in Puerto Rico. We welcome you to taste everything in the menu from our exotic rolls to our famous sushi pizza. For the children and all the young at heart, let our chefs entertain you with their cooking skills on the Teppanyaki tables, where a wonderful Teppanyaki show delights diners while the most delicious hibachi dishes are prepared, such as shrimps, scallops, filet mignon, lobster and many others.

The Whiskey Room

For all true lovers of whisky, the Whisky Room is a luxurious, intimate and exquisite space located at the East Wine Bar next to East Asian Bistro. Our elegant room can accommodate up to 10 guests, designed to provide the right ambiance for enjoying the company of your friends & family or to have a private business meeting.

The Wine Bar

There’s a whole new aspect to "Wine me & Dine me" you have yet to discover at Guaynabo’s East Asian Bistro. Whether you decide to enjoy a glass before your meal, or after you’ve had your fill of exotic and expertly prepared culinary offerings, The Wine Room is just the right place for indulging in some lounge time with friends, sharing a couple of decanters of your tried and true favorite hot or cold sake, or go ahead and sample the excellent wine variety available from our cellar.

Our special menu is sure to have something to please every palate, with a variety of Artisan draft beer, sake, wine, or ask the bartender to concoct your particular drink mix. It’s all a matter of good taste and we have that in spades!

The Activity Room

The classically demure East Asian Bistro’s Activity Room is now available to host celebrations of all kinds, business dinners, family occasions, bridal receptions, anniversaries and any other type of event for which a spacious and comfortable dining area is required.

Decorated with an array of understated beautiful artwork, the Activity Room can accommodate up to 180 diners in a quietly elegant setting that provides your private party with an extra level of privacy from our main dining areas.

The highest quality in every offering, impeccable service and a discriminating attention to detail are distinguishing factors to be expected when you select this venue for your gathering. Contact us for more information on availability, choice of menus or a consultation regarding your special event.
