East Asian Bistro

If you’re searching for the best sushi in Puerto Rico, you’ll find it at East Asian Bistro in the heart of Guaynabo City. This beautifully designed restaurant serves all types of flavors from the Far East. When you arrive, you’ll be greeted by the impressive granite "Heavenly Gateway" where the eternally Happy Buddha sits at the cascading fountain and watches over the entrance, beckoning guests to make a wish.
